Passive

Runic Blade

Riven's abilities charge her blade for 6 seconds, causing her to deal 25 / 30 / 35 / 40 / 45 / 50% of her total attack damage as bonus physical damage on her next basic attack. The amount increases at level 3, and every 3 levels from there on. Riven can store up to 3 charges, and can only expend one at a time.
